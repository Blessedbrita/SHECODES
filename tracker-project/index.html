HOW ARE YOU MANAGING YOUR POCKET?

INCOME

Managing your finances is an essential aspect of financial freedom. Handling it wisely will save you from financial stress. Creating a monthly budget is the best way to keep track of your finances.
Top tip: It is to create a budget that reflects your goals and financial situation.

EXPENSES

Effectively managing your finances is crucial as this limit unnecessary spending. The best approach is prioritising your needs first, and being mindful of your wants. Review your monthly expenses to keep track and identify non-essential expenditure that can be cut. Reassess your financial situation and adjust your budget to your financial goals.

SAVINGS

Rule 1: As your income increases, increase in investing and savings! Managing your savings is crucial for building financial security and achieving your long-term goals. Here, it is where you need to befriend a financial advisor that will help you with plans that fit your financial needs.
